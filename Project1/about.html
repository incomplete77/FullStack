Welcome to the 2023 Music Competition!

Our annual music competition is back, and we are thrilled to announce that we are now accepting entries for the 2023 edition. This year's competition promises to be bigger and better than ever before, with more categories and bigger prizes.

The competition is open to all musicians, regardless of genre, age or location. Whether you are a solo performer, part of a band or a classical ensemble, we welcome your entry. Our aim is to provide a platform for emerging artists to showcase their talents and gain exposure, while also recognizing established musicians for their contributions to the industry.

There are a total of six categories in this year's competition: classical music, jazz, pop, rock, electronic and world music. Each category will have its own judging panel, made up of experienced professionals in that particular genre. The judges will be looking for exceptional musicianship, creativity and originality in each entry.

Prizes will be awarded to the top three entries in each category, with the first prize winner receiving a cash prize, as well as the opportunity to perform at a prestigious concert venue. The second and third prize winners will also receive cash prizes.

To enter the competition, simply visit our website and fill out the entry form. You will need to provide a video of your performance, which can be uploaded to our website or submitted via a video hosting platform such as YouTube or Vimeo. Entries close on the 30th of April, so be sure to get your submission in before then.

We are excited to see the amazing talent that will be showcased in this year's competition, and we look forward to welcoming you to the stage. Good luck to all entrants!
